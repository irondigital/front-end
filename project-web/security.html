🛍️ Tips for Safe Online Shopping

  • 🔹 Use strong and unique passwords for your Flipkart account.
  • 🔹 Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for extra account protection.
  • 🔹 Avoid using public Wi-Fi when making purchases.
  • 🔹 Verify product sellers and read reviews carefully.
  • 🔹 Be cautious of phishing emails asking for personal info.
  • 🔹 Regularly review your orders and account activity.
