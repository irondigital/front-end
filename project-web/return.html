Flipkart Returns Center

Everything you need to know about returning your products

📜 Return Policy

You can return most items within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

  • ✔️ No return on digital products, gift cards, and personal care items
  • ✔️ Refunds processed within 7 working days after the return is received
  • ✔️ Return shipping charges may apply for certain cases

🚀 How to Initiate a Return

  1. Step 1: Login to your Flipkart account.
  2. Step 2: Go to My Orders section.
  3. Step 3: Select the order you want to return and click "Return".
  4. Step 4: Choose the reason for return and submit your request.
  5. Step 5: Our support will guide you through the next steps.

You will receive an email confirmation once the return is successfully initiated.

❓ Return FAQs

Q1: Can I return a product after 30 days?

No, returns are accepted only within 30 days from delivery date unless otherwise specified.

Q2: Will I get a full refund?

Yes, as long as the product is returned unused and in original condition. Shipping charges may not be refunded.

Q3: How long does the return process take?

Refunds are typically processed within 7 working days after Flipkart receives the returned item.
